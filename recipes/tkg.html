卵かけご飯 (Tamago Kake Gohan) – Silver Spoon

A classic Japanese comfort food: hot rice topped with a raw egg and a splash of soy sauce.


My version:

Ingredients

  • 1 pasteurised egg
  • 1 bowl of hot rice
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare a bowl of hot rice.
  2. Crack the egg on top of the rice.
  3. Drizzle with soy sauce.
  4. Mix well and enjoy!
