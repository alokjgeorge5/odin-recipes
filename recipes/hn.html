ハムラーメン (Ham Ramen) – Ponyo

A simple and comforting noodle soup, just like in the movie Ponyo!


My version:

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of instant noodles
  • Water
  • 1 slice of cooked ham
  • 1 egg
  • Green onion (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook the instant noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Pour the noodles and broth into a bowl.
  3. Top with a slice of ham, a boiled egg (halved), and green onion if you like.
  4. Enjoy your Ponyo-style ramen!
